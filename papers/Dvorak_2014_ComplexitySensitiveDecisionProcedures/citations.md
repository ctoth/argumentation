## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Dung (1995): foundational AF formalism and semantics.
- Besnard and Doutre (2004): SAT encodings for first-level AF reasoning, reused as a base layer.
- Ordyniak and Szeider (2011): tractable fragment augmentation and graph-distance perspective.
- Egly, Gaggl, and Woltran (2010): ASP encodings and solver baseline relevant to implementation comparison.
- Dvorak, Gaggl, Wallner, and Woltran (2011a): ASP-based argumentation system used as a practical comparator.
