## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. Dung 1995 is the foundational source for abstract argumentation frameworks and acceptability semantics.
2. Caminada and Gabbay 2009 provides the complete-labelling characterization used by PrefSat.
3. Nofal, Dunne, and Atkinson 2012 is the strongest dedicated preferred-extension enumeration comparator in this paper.
4. Egly, Gaggl, and Woltran 2008 introduces ASPARTIX, the main ASP translation baseline.
5. Wallner, Weissenbacher, and Woltran 2013 is the closest follow-up for advanced SAT techniques in abstract argumentation.
