Collects the primary ICCMA 2023 solver and benchmark descriptions across exact, approximate, dynamic, and ABA tracks. Its most transferable techniques are Crustabri and mu-toksia's persistent SAT state, Fudge and ASPforABA's task-specific hard-problem routes, k-Solutions' witness validation, and Crusti_g2io's tunable community benchmarks. It gives this project concrete operational contracts for solver-call bounds, route selection, retained state, and topology-sensitive testing.
