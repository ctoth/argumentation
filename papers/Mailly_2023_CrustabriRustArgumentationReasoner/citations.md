## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Lagniez, Lonca, and Mailly (2015), CoQuiAAS:** predecessor architecture and encoding baseline for understanding Crustabri’s changes.
- **Niskanen and Järvisalo (2020), mu-toksia:** detailed incremental-SAT and CEGAR task decomposition independently corroborated by the 2023 description.
- **Thimm, Cerutti, and Vallati (2021), skeptical preferred reasoning:** the direct DS-PR characterization used by Fudge to avoid preferred-extension enumeration.
- **Lehtonen, Wallner, and Järvisalo (2021), incremental ABA ASP:** full algorithmic source behind ASPforABA’s multi-shot Clingo route.
- **Lehtonen et al. (2023), ABA-induced AF size/complexity:** formal basis for AcbAr’s polynomial ABA-to-AF construction.
