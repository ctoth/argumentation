## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Dunne et al. 2011 [2]:** Source for weighted argument systems, inconsistency budgets, and complexity results that ConArg targets with CP.
- **Bistarelli et al. 1997 [11] and Bistarelli 2004 [12]:** Core semiring/soft-constraint foundation for the SCSP representation.
- **Dung 1995 [5]:** Canonical AF semantics used as the baseline for all crisp encodings.
- **Egly et al. 2008 [1] and Dvorak et al. 2011 [33]:** ASPARTIX and answer-set-programming comparison baseline.
- **Amgoud and Devred 2011 [37]:** Closest related work encoding AFs as CSPs, but focused on qualitative preferences and without ConArg's weighted implementation/tests.
