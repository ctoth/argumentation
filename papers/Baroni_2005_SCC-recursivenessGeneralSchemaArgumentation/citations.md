## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. Dung 1995 [9] is the foundational abstract-argumentation paper; SCC-recursiveness is proven to subsume all its admissibility-based semantics.
2. Baroni & Giacomin ECSQARU 2003 [4] is the origin of CF2, the most important new semantics introduced here and now a mainstream ICCMA task.
3. Baroni, Giacomin & Guida 2000 [6] ("Extending abstract argumentation systems theory") is the authors' earlier groundwork on the cycle problem.
4. Baroni & Giacomin 2004 [8] on formalizing skepticism is the basis for the paper's proposed future comparison of SCC-recursive semantics.
5. Pollock [14,18] supplies the "puzzling" odd-cycle critique that motivates the whole investigation.
