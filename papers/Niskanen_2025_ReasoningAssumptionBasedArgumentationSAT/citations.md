## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Rankooh, M. F., and Rintanen, J. 2022. Propositional encodings of acyclicity and reachability by using vertex elimination. AAAI.** — The vertex-elimination encoding that powers SAT-VE, the best-performing variant. Essential to understand/reimplement the compact acyclicity encoding.
- **Gebser, Janhunen, and Rintanen 2014. SAT modulo graphs: Acyclicity. JELIA.** — Basis of the SAT-ACYC graph-based acyclicity propagator.
- **Gebser, Kaufmann, and Schaub 2012. Conflict-driven answer set solving. Artif. Intell.** — Source-pointer / unfounded-set technique behind SAT-UFS.
- **Fazekas et al. 2024. Satisfiability modulo user propagators. JAIR.** — IPASIR-UP, the interface that makes SAT-ACYC and SAT-UFS implementable in CaDiCaL.
- **Lehtonen, Wallner, and Järvisalo 2021a/2021b. Declarative algorithms / incremental ASP for ABA.** — ASPforABA, the state-of-the-art baseline this paper competes against.
