## Key Citations for Follow-up

- Bondarenko et al. (1997): original ABA framework and default-reasoning basis.
- Dung (1995): abstract argumentation foundations and the fundamental lemma behind several preference/grounded discussions.
- Dimopoulos et al. (2002), Dunne (2009), and Dvořák & Dunne (2018): complexity background for ABA and argumentation semantics.
- Bao et al. (2017) and Cyras & Toni (2016a/2016b/2016c): ABA+ preference formalism and ABAPLUS comparison point.
- Lehtonen et al. (2017, 2019, 2020): the authors' surrounding implementation line from ABA-to-AF translation through direct ASP and ASPIC+ extensions.
