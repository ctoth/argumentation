## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **Bondarenko et al. 1997 [1]:** The abstract ABA/default-reasoning framework whose semantics and concrete instances are analyzed here.
- **Dung 1991 [8]:** Source for the admissibility/preferred argumentation semantics generalized in the paper.
- **Dimopoulos, Nebel, Toni 1999/2000 [4, 5]:** Earlier conference papers combined into this full article with complete proofs.
- **Sacca 1997 [28]:** DATALOG expressiveness result that motivates and supports LP hardness results for preferred/admissible semantics.
- **Eiter and Gottlob 1992 [9]:** Related AEL semantics that the authors identify as an open comparison point for admissible/preferred AEL.
